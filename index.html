What we do

I use ArcGIS Pro, spatial analysis, and automation to optimize environmental data for conservation and land management. By integrating geospatial technology with sustainability strategies, I contribute to innovative ecological solutions. Currently, I’m also developing a web platform to centralize key information on restoration projects, agroforestry initiatives, protected area documents, and upcoming environmental data. This platform aims to improve accessibility and decision-making in conservation efforts.
